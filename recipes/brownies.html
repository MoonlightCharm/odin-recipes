Cheesecake Brownies

Description

These cheesecake brownies are a jazzed-up version of an out-of-the-box brownie mix with an easy cheesecake topping.

Ingredients

  • Brownie mix
  • Water
  • Vegetable oil
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Prepare brownie mix
  3. Add cheesecake topping
  4. Bake in oven
